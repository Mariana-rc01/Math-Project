About us

Welcome to our website, "Math Project", your gateway to the world of mathematical analysis and probability. This platform was created and developed by Mariana Rocha.

Our Team

The part of mathematical analysis was developed by Mariana Rocha, who holds a degree in mathematics and is currently pursuing a degree in computer engineering at the University of Minho. You can find Mariana's GitHub profile here and her LinkedIn profile here.

Project Background

This website was developed as part of the "Inclusive Mathematics Education Based on Digital Learning" (IMEDIL) project, created under the ERASMUS+ program. The project was supported by a three-month research scholarship during the summer of 2023.
